What do I do?

This is a Hot or Cold number guessing game. It goes like this:

  • 1. I (the computer), pick a random secret number between 1 to 100.
  • 2. You (the player), need to keep guessing until you correctly guess the secret number.
  • 3. You will get feedback on how close ("hot") or far ("cold") your guess is from the actual number.

So, are you ready? Good luck!
